ABOUT



Who Are We?


Health Diary is nutrition application set up and developed, based on information from re-known health organisations like WHO(World Health Organisation),FAO(Food and Agriculture Organisation –of the United Nations)etc.to strengthen the maternal knowledge ,attitude and complementary feeding practices of caregivers’ of children aged 6-59 months. It aims to improve dietary habits and food choices, in order to reverse the under nutrition in children and improve their nutritional diagnosis. It will also promote desirable food behaviour and nutritional practices and Increase the diversity of the types of foods consumed by children 6-59 months. Caregivers are given nutrition education and are equipped with the relevant skills as recommended by the World Health Organisation(WHO),as the application’s main focus is on , child immunization, maternal nutrition knowledge, food consistency, meal frequency, amount of complimentary food needed and use of Vitamin-mineral Supplementation for children under five years. If it is adopted in hospitals and health care centres, the application might also help in providing feedback to the ministry of health.


Mission:


To brief and educate the general public ,more so first time mothers and caregivers on not only nutrition complimentary feeding practices available to infants and breast feeding mothers but also sensitize knowledge on the health services available to the infants in both the government and private health facilities in the country at large.



Vision:


To increase the utilization of the application and advance delivery of nutrition knowledge based health and nutrition services, hence thrusting the country towards achieving the Millennium Development Goals (MDG’s), reducing child mortality to be precise (MDG 4).
